# Micro-SaaS Pricing Matrix & ROI Calculator 📈

A financial modeling dashboard designed for indie hackers and student founders. It visualizes the impact of pricing strategies, user growth, and infrastructure costs on the long-term viability of a software product.

## 🌟 Features

1. **Tiered Pricing Builder**: Configure monthly subscription costs for your 'Starter', 'Pro', and 'Enterprise' plans.
2. **Interactive Variables**: Use responsive sliders to adjust Total Active Users, Customer Churn Rate, and Monthly Server Costs to simulate different business scenarios.
3. **Distribution Weighting**: Easily adjust the percentage of your user base sitting in the 'Free' tier vs. 'Paid' tiers.
4. **Live Financial Metrics**: Instantly calculates and displays:
   - **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**
   - **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**
   - **LTV (Customer Lifetime Value)**
   - **Net Profit**
5. **Custom SVG Projection Charts**: Features a 12-month revenue trajectory bar chart, rendered entirely via native JavaScript algorithms without the need for external charting libraries.

## 🚀 Usage

1. Open the application from the central dashboard.
2. Adjust the pricing numbers in the configuration panel.
3. Slide the "Total Users" and "Churn Rate" sliders to mimic an optimistic or pessimistic growth scenario.
4. Watch the Dashboard KPI Cards instantly recalculate your MRR and Net Margins.
5. Review the custom generated SVG chart on the right side to determine if your Micro-SaaS covers its infrastructure costs over a 12-month horizon.
