# Student Scholarship & Internship Matcher Dashboard 🎓

A comprehensive career planner designed to help students discover and track scholarships and internship opportunities perfectly suited for their academic profiles.

## 🌟 Features

1. **Academic Filter Panel**: Define your academic profile by entering your GPA, Field of Study, and specific tags (e.g. "Merit-based", "Tech").
2. **Dynamic Matching Engine**: Instantly filters a rich database of mock scholarships and internships to only show opportunities you qualify for.
3. **Application Kanban Pipeline**: Track your applications using an interactive drag-and-drop Kanban board with standard progression swimlanes:
   - Saved
   - Applied
   - Interviewing
   - Accepted
4. **Local Progress Caching**: The state of your Kanban board is saved to your browser's local storage, ensuring you never lose your application tracking data.

## 🚀 Usage

1. Open the application dashboard and look at the **Filter Panel** on the left.
2. Enter your GPA and filter by your specific Major/Field of Study to refine the **Opportunities Grid**.
3. Found an interesting opportunity? Click **Save** on the card.
4. Open the **Kanban Application Tracker** tab. You'll see your saved opportunity in the "Saved" lane.
5. As you progress through the application lifecycle, physically **drag and drop** the card into the "Applied", "Interviewing", and "Accepted" lanes!
6. Refresh the page to verify your application pipeline was safely preserved via `localStorage`.
